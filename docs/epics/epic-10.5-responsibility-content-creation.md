## Content Standards

All articles must follow `/docs/bmm/data/documentation-standards.md`:

**Frontmatter Requirements**:
```yaml
---
title: "Article Title - Descriptive Subtitle"
slug: article-slug
path: responsibility
publishDate: YYYY-MM-DD
category: Legal & Compliance / Governance / Best Practices
tldr: "1-2 sentence summary of regulatory requirements or governance approach"
tags:
  - jurisdiction-tag
  - regulation-tag
  - compliance-tag
  - governance-tag
relatedConcepts:
  - resp-X (related responsibility articles)
crossPathRefs:
  - path: risk
    articles: [risk-X, risk-Y]
  - path: future
    articles: [future-X]
  - path: terminology
    articles: [term-X]
examples:
  - example-case-study-slug
  - example-enforcement-action-slug
sources:
  - title: "Official regulation text"
    url: "https://..."
  - title: "Regulatory guidance document"
    url: "https://..."
---
```

**Content Structure**:
1. Overview (200-300 words) - What is this regulation/framework and why it matters
2. Regulatory Framework / Key Requirements (1200-1800 words) - Detailed analysis
3. Compliance Implications (400-600 words) - What organizations must do
4. Enforcement and Penalties (300-400 words) - Consequences of non-compliance
5. Practical Guidance (300-400 words) - How to comply effectively

**Writing Style**:
- Professional legal tone
- Clear explanations of complex legal concepts
- Cite specific legal provisions and authorities
- Include compliance timelines and deadlines
- Provide actionable guidance
- Balance legal accuracy with accessibility
