## Content Standards

**Frontmatter**:
```yaml
---
title: "Article Title - Descriptive Subtitle"
slug: article-slug
path: risk
publishDate: YYYY-MM-DD
category: Risk & Safety
tldr: "1-2 sentence risk summary"
tags: [risk-tag, safety-tag]
relatedConcepts: [risk-X, risk-Y]
crossPathRefs:
  - path: responsibility
    articles: [resp-X]
  - path: terminology
    articles: [term-X]
---
```

**Structure**:
1. Introduction (200-300 words) - What is this risk
2. Risk Analysis (800-1000 words) - Detailed examination
3. Real-World Examples (400-600 words) - Case studies
4. Mitigation Strategies (400-600 words) - Best practices
5. Key Takeaways (150-200 words)
