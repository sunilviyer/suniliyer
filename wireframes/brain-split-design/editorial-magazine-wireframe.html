✅ Benefits of Editorial Magazine Style

  • Natural flow: Content and images work together, not fighting
  • Mobile-friendly: Grid collapses to stack naturally on small screens
  • Sophisticated: Feels like a premium publication, not a generic website
  • Contextual: Images support nearby content rather than random breaks
  • Flexible: Easy to adjust layouts per section without major refactor
